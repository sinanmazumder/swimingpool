Gas Heater

Is it early spring or fall, and you’re dreaming of getting in your pool? If so, we can help you! Here at Round Rock Pool Pros, we are proud and honored to professionally install heaters so our customers can extend their swimming season and enhance their pool experience.

If you’re considering a pool heater, here are a few things you should know about these types of units

Benefits of a Pool Heater

These units are very efficient at quickly heating the water in your pool. Because the gas unit is able to increase the water temperature so fast, it keeps the system from having to run as long. Plus, the price of the gas or oil for these types of units are relatively inexpensive, keeping your pool costs low.
